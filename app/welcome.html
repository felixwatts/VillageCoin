is a currency and monetary system owned and run by Reddit users. It has some interesting features, including:

  • Democracy
  • Basic Income
  • Automatic taxation

The citizens of control and manage the currency through referendums, doing things like:

  • Creating money
  • Setting tax policy
  • Spending public funds

Anyone can send and receive tokens, just like any ERC20 token, but to gain voting rights and other benefits within the system you need to become a citizen. Its open to anyone with a Reddit account and a decent level of karma
