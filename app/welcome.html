VillageCoin

Welcome

VillageCoin is a proof-of-concept democratic monetary system. It is a currency in which monetary policy is decided democratically by the users on a one person; one vote basis

Unlike most currencies, VillageCoin implements a one person; one account policy, which means that each citizen can have at most one VillageCoin bank account. This allows some interesting features of VillageCoin including:

  • Democracy
  • Basic Income
  • Automatic taxation

Access to the VillageCoin economy is controlled by the VillageCoin GateKeeper Committee. It is their job to enforce the one person; one account policy

Account holders in VillageCoin are called citizens. Citizens control and manage VillageCoin through a democratic system of proposals and referendums, doing things like:

  • Creating money
  • Setting tax policy
  • Spending public funds
  • Electing and dismissing gatekeepers
